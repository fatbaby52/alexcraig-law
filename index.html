About Alex Craig

Experienced Counsel.
Compassionate Advocate.

After graduating from McGeorge School of Law in 2009, I spent nine years serving as in-house counsel for two different companies. This experience gave me a unique perspective on how to provide practical, business-minded legal solutions while never losing sight of the human element.

Now in private practice, I bring that same dedication and personalized approach to individuals and families throughout California's Central Coast and Central Valley. Whether you're planning for your family's future, navigating the immigration system, facing criminal charges, or recovering from an injury, I'm here to guide you through every step.
