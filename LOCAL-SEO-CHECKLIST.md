## 2. Review generation cadence

- [ ] Create the GBP review short link (Share > Ask for reviews) and save it
- [ ] Habit: at every successful matter close (buyback completed, severance negotiated), send one text/email with the review link within 48 hours; ask for one sentence about responsiveness and clarity, never about outcomes
- [ ] Target: 1-2 reviews per month, steadily; a burst of 10 reviews in one week looks manufactured
- [ ] Respond to every review within a week (thank; never confirm someone was a client in your reply; never discuss case details)
- [ ] Never incentivize reviews (violates Google TOS and risks bar advertising issues)
